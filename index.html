Dear Snoor, ❤️

Pata hai...

Kabhi kabhi main sochta hoon ki meri life ka sabse beautiful surprise tum thi.

Agar kisi ne mujhe pehle bola hota ki ek random Telegram group se meri life ka sabse important person milega...

To shayad main kabhi believe nahi karta.

Lekin aaj main sirf itna jaanta hoon...

Thank you meri life me aane ke liye.

Thank you har us Good Morning message ke liye jisse mera din achha shuru hota hai.

Thank you meri har chhoti badi baat patiently sunne ke liye.

Thank you meri tension samajhne ke liye.

Aur sabse zyada...

Thank you meri care karne ke liye. ❤️

Shayad tumhare liye ye sab normal ho...

Lekin mere liye jab tum poochti ho...

"Khana khaya?"

"Ghar pahunch gaye?"

"Apna dhyan rakhna."

To ye sirf words nahi hote...

Ye mujhe feel karate hain ki koi hai jo dil se meri fikr karta hai.

Aur pata hai...

Tumhari har notification dekhkar jo smile aati hai...

Us feeling ko shayad words me kabhi explain hi nahi kar paunga.

Main perfect nahi hoon.

Kabhi galtiyan karta hoon.

Kabhi gussa bhi aa jata hai.

Kabhi samajhne me der lag jaati hai.

Lekin ek baat dil se promise karta hoon...

Main kabhi jaan-bujhkar tumhe hurt nahi karna chahta.

Aur agar kabhi meri kisi baat se tum hurt hui ho...

To dil se...

❤️ I'm really sorry. ❤️

Main bas itna chahta hoon ki...

Life chahe kitni bhi busy ho jaye...

Kitni bhi problems aa jaye...

Hum ek dusre ka haath kabhi na chhodein.

Har mushkil saath me face karein.

Har khushi saath me celebrate karein.

Aur har naye din ki shuruaat ek dusre ke saath karein.

Mujhe future me bahut kuch nahi chahiye...

Bas itna chahiye...

Ki ek din hum dono peeche mudkar dekhein aur smile karke bolein...

❤️ "Humne har phase saath me nikala... aur aaj bhi saath hain."

Aur agar kabhi life tumhe thaka de...

Ya tumhe lage ki sab kuch difficult ho raha hai...

To bas ek baat hamesha yaad rakhna...

Aaj bhi.
